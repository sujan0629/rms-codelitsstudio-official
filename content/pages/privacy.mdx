# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

Thank you for choosing **cls.RmS™**, the Restaurant Management System built by **Codelits Studio Pvt. Ltd.** We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains:

- **What data** we collect  
- **How** we use it  
- **When** and **why** we share it  
- **Your rights** over your information  

---

## 1. Data We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you register for cls.RmS™, we collect:
- **Name**, **email address**, **phone number**  
- **Business details** (restaurant name, location)

### 1.2 Usage Data
To provide and improve cls.RmS™, we log:
- **Login timestamps**, **features used**, **page views**  
- **Device and browser type**, **IP address**, **geolocation (city level)**  

### 1.3 Transaction Data
If you process orders or payments through cls.RmS™, we collect:
- **Order details** (items, quantities, prices)  
- **Payment method**, **transaction ID**, **billing address** (if applicable)

### 1.4 Cookies & Tracking
cls.RmS™ uses cookies and similar technologies to:
- Keep you logged in  
- Remember your preferences  
- Analyze usage trends  

You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not function correctly.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

1. **Provide & maintain** cls.RmS™ features  
2. **Authenticate** and **secure** your account  
3. **Process** orders and payments  
4. **Personalize** your experience (e.g. language, display settings)  
5. **Improve** our product via analytics and feedback  
6. **Communicate** with you (support, updates, newsletters)

---

## 3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do **not** sell your personal data. We may share it with:

- **Service Providers** (payment processors, email services) under confidentiality agreements  
- **Legal Authorities** if required by law (e.g., court order)  
- **Business Transfers** in the event of a merger or acquisition (under similar privacy obligations)

---

## 4. Data Retention & Security

- We retain your account data **as long as your account is active** or as needed to provide services.  
- Usage logs are kept **up to 24 months**, after which they are anonymized or deleted.  
- We implement industry‑standard security measures (encryption, firewalls, access controls) to protect your data.

---

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data  
- **Correct** inaccurate information  
- **Request deletion** of your account and data  
- **Object to or restrict** certain processing  
- **Export** your data in a portable format  

To exercise these rights, please contact us at **privacy@codelitsstudio.com**.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

cls.RmS™ is **not** intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child’s information has been provided, please contact us to have it removed.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will become effective when we post the revised policy here, with the “Last updated” date at the top.
